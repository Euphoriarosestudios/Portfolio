Welcome to My Story

Hi, I'm Marwan Salah-Bennani, a passionate artist and creative soul who loves to bring ideas to life. My journey in the world of art has been a beautiful mix of discovery, learning, and endless creativity.

From digital painting to character design, and even 2D animations, I believe art is a powerful way to communicate emotions and tell stories. My goal is to inspire others through my work and continue exploring new ways to express myself creatively.

When I'm not creating art, you'll find me exploring nature, reading fantasy novels, or diving into the latest tech trends. Let's create something amazing together!

Meet The

The Artist

As a Digital Illustrator, I strive to create eye-catching and awe-inspiring pieces of art that bring joy and inspiration to those who view them. Whether you're looking for a stunning piece of art for your office space or an illustration for your next project, I use my skills and expertise in Adobe Photoshop to bring your ideas to life. Since I began painting in 2020, I've been constantly learning and innovating, and I'm excited to share my passion with you.

The Animator

As an artist, I strive to create unique and inspiring artwork. Recently, I've added bone animations to my list of creative avenues. Using Creature2d from Krystal Moon and Unity, I take a single image and bring it to life through motion. These animations are a natural progression within digital paintings because the images used are the digital paintings I would make. If you're interested in exploring this innovative approach to art, let's work together

The Character Designer

Being a digital artist allows me to share my passion for character design in a unique way. Every character has a life story, and by creating digital paintings, I am able to help that story come to life. Using digital painting software, I create characters with unique features and personalities. I can spend hours on a single piece, making sure that every brush stroke is precise and capturing the essence of the character. Every piece is a creative challenge, and I enjoy the process of bringing new ideas to life.
